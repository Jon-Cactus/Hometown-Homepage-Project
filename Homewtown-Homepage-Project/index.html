Visit Tachikawa

Enjoy beautiful sights and endless fun in this western pocket of Tokyo!

Some nice activities for a day in Tachikawa:

Visit historic Showa Kinen Park

This beautiful park was built on what used to be an American occupied military base

After the park, check out the Tamashin Art Museum!

This perfectly-placed museum is surrounded by a modern shopping mall and plenty of restaurants

After dinner, catch a ride on the Tama monorail

This monorail leads to many other interesting locations, but if you time your ride right, you can catch a beautiful view of Mt. Fuji!
